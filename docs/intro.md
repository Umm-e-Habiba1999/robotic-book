## Welcome to the Future of AI Education

Welcome to the AI-Native Technical Textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook represents a new approach to learning‚Äîa truly AI-native educational experience that combines rigorous technical content with interactive, retrieval-enhanced learning tools.

**Physical AI** represents a paradigm shift from traditional digital AI systems that process information in isolation to embodied systems that must navigate, understand, and manipulate the physical world. These systems face unique challenges: they must account for physics, handle uncertainty in sensor data, manage real-time constraints, and operate safely in human environments.

This textbook is designed for advanced students who already possess foundational knowledge in AI and programming. We assume familiarity with basic machine learning concepts, linear algebra, and programming principles, allowing us to dive directly into the complexities of embodied intelligence.
